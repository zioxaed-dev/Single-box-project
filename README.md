# Single-box-project
Just a simple android program, like **Soundpad**. In the program you can play music from **your device** and from [Myinstants](https://www.myinstants.com/ru/index/ua/). Also you can **save liked audio** and play it in **Saved Audio page**.

### Warn!
The program in **beta version** so his have a some bugs. 

**P.S**: grant permission for photos and videos separately in the app settings.
